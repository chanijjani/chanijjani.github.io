# Upload Your Resume PDF Here

To enable the PDF download functionality on your website:

1. **Upload your resume PDF** to this `public` folder
2. **Rename it to** `Chanhee_Lee_Resume.pdf` (or update the filename in `src/app/page.tsx`)
3. The download button will automatically work once the PDF is in place
